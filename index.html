What is USDT Sender?

USDT Sender is a system designed to explain and simulate how Tether (USDT) transfers work across blockchain networks like TRC20, ERC20, and BEP20.

Supported Networks

  • TRC20 - Low fees & fast speed
  • ERC20 - High security
  • BEP20 - Low cost & fast transactions
